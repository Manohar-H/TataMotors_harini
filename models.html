Explore Tata Motors' Top 3 Models

Tata Motors is renowned for its innovative designs, strong safety focus, and environmentally friendly technology. Whether you’re seeking a compact city car, a rugged SUV, or a sustainable electric vehicle, Tata Motors has a model for every need.

1. TATA NEXON

The First 5-Star Safety Rated Indian Car

The Tata Nexon is a compact SUV that has redefined safety standards in India. Launched in 2017, it became the first Indian vehicle to receive a 5-star safety rating from Global NCAP. The Nexon is available in both petrol and diesel variants, featuring a 1.2L turbocharged petrol engine or a 1.5L Revotorq diesel engine. The Nexon EV variant offers a greener alternative with an all-electric drivetrain.

  1. Engine Options: 1.2L turbocharged petrol, 1.5L diesel engine.
  2. Safety: 5-star Global NCAP rating, dual airbags, ABS with EBD, ESP, and a reinforced steel structure.
  3. Infotainment: 7-inch touchscreen with Android Auto, Apple CarPlay, and voice commands.
  4. Fuel Efficiency: 17-21 km/l depending on the variant.

2. TATA HARRIER

Bold Design, Powerful Performance

The Tata Harrier is a mid-size SUV known for its aggressive styling and premium features. It’s built on the OmegaArc platform, derived from Land Rover’s D8 architecture, and offers excellent off-road capabilities alongside luxury features.

  1. Engine: 2.0L Kryotec turbocharged diesel engine, delivering 170 PS of power and 350 Nm of torque.
  2. Transmission: 6-speed manual and automatic options.
  3. Infotainment: 8.8-inch touchscreen infotainment system with JBL audio, Android Auto, and Apple CarPlay.
  4. Safety: 6 airbags, ESP, hill descent control, and roll-over mitigation.

3. TATA ALTROZ

The Gold Standard of Hatchbacks

The Tata Altroz is a premium hatchback known for its futuristic design and best-in-class safety standards. It offers two engine options: a 1.2L Revotron petrol engine and a 1.5L Revotorq diesel engine, both providing balanced performance and fuel efficiency.

  1. Engine Options: 1.2L petrol, 1.5L diesel.
  2. Design: Tata's Impact 2.0 Design language gives the Altroz a bold and dynamic exterior.
  3. Safety: 5-star Global NCAP rating, dual airbags, ABS with EBD, corner stability control, and rear parking sensors.
  4. Technology: 7-inch touchscreen infotainment with Android Auto, Apple CarPlay, and connected car features.
